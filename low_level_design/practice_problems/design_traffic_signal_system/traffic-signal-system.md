# Design Traffic Signal System 

### Requirements

1. The traffic signal system should manage the flow of vehicles at intersections with multiple roads.

2. It should support various signal types, including red, yellow, and green lights.

3. Signal durations must be configurable and adjustable based on real-time traffic conditions.

4. The system should ensure smooth and safe transitions between signal states.

5. It must detect and appropriately respond to emergency situations, such as ambulances or fire trucks approaching the intersection.

6. The system should be scalable and extensible to accommodate future enhancements and additional features.

### Core Objects

1. **Signal**- represents enum type of signal i.e Red, Green and Yellow
